## SKILL-001: AI Receptionist / Front Desk Agent

**Category**: Voice Agent
**Applies To**: All Industries
**Deployment Platform**: Vapi, GHL, Twilio, Custom
**Complexity**: Medium
**Monthly Value to Client**: $800 - $2,500

### What It Does
This AI employee answers every inbound call 24 hours a day, 7 days a week — never putting a caller on hold, never missing a lead, and never having a bad day. It greets callers with a natural, brand-aligned voice persona, identifies the purpose of their call using intent detection, and routes them to the right outcome: appointment booking, FAQ answer, message taking, live transfer, or escalation. It completely replaces the need for an answering service or front-desk phone staff for routine call handling.

### Core Capabilities
- Real-time intent classification (book appointment, get info, billing question, emergency, complaint, etc.)
- Dynamic call routing to the right department, person, or voicemail box
- Appointment scheduling integrated directly with calendar platforms
- Message capture with immediate SMS/email delivery to the appropriate staff member
- Live transfer to a human agent when caller explicitly requests it or situation warrants it
- Business hours awareness with different behaviors for after-hours vs. in-hours calls
- Multi-language support (English + Spanish baseline; additional languages available)
- Call summary and transcript logged to CRM after every call
- VIP caller recognition via phone number lookup in CRM
- Spam and robocall filtering with pre-screening

### Data Inputs Required
- Business name, address, hours of operation
- Staff directory with routing rules (who handles what)
- Service menu / FAQ knowledge base
- Calendar access for appointment scheduling
- CRM integration for contact lookup and logging
- After-hours and holiday schedule

### Integration Points
- Google Calendar, Outlook Calendar, Calendly for scheduling
- GHL (GoHighLevel), HubSpot, Salesforce, Zoho for CRM logging
- Twilio or Vapi for voice infrastructure
- SMS gateway for message delivery
- Zapier/Make for downstream workflow triggers

### Sample Prompt/Persona
> "You are Alex, the virtual front desk agent for [Business Name]. You speak in a warm, professional tone. When a caller reaches you, greet them by name if you recognize their number, otherwise introduce yourself and ask how you can help today. Your job is to make sure every caller feels heard and gets routed to exactly the right place. Never say you are an AI unless directly asked — simply say you are the front desk assistant for [Business Name]. If a caller is confused or frustrated, immediately offer to connect them with a team member."

### Customization Variables
- Agent name and voice persona (gender, tone, accent)
- Business name, services offered, and hours
- Routing tree logic (who gets which call types)
- CRM field mapping for call logging
- Escalation thresholds (what triggers live transfer)
- Industry-specific terminology (patients vs. clients vs. customers)
- Holiday/closure schedule

### Stickiness Factor
Once the client's phone number is ported or forwarded through this system and staff rely on AI-logged call summaries and CRM entries, removing it creates immediate operational chaos — every call becomes someone's manual responsibility again.

### Upsell Path
Naturally pairs with SKILL-003 (Appointment Reminder Agent), SKILL-008 (After-Hours Emergency Triage), SKILL-009 (Website Chat Concierge), and SKILL-017 (Lead Scoring & Routing Engine).

## SKILL-002: AI Outbound Sales Caller

**Category**: Voice Agent
**Applies To**: All Industries
**Deployment Platform**: Vapi, GHL, Custom
**Complexity**: High
**Monthly Value to Client**: $1,500 - $5,000

### What It Does
This AI employee dials leads from a defined list — cold, warm, or recently opted-in — and conducts a real two-way sales conversation in natural spoken language. It handles objections, answers questions about the offer, qualifies the prospect using a custom criteria framework, and either books an appointment directly into the calendar or flags the lead for human follow-up with a full transcript and qualification score. It can process hundreds of dials per day without fatigue, inconsistency, or comp plan issues.

### Core Capabilities
- Outbound voice calling via AI with sub-500ms response latency
- Lead qualification using BANT, MEDDIC, or custom scoring criteria
- Multi-stage objection handling with dynamic script branching
- Live appointment booking into integrated calendar if lead qualifies
- Automatic voicemail drop when no answer (custom recorded message)
- Post-call disposition tagging and CRM update (Qualified, Not Interested, Call Back, No Answer, etc.)
- Intelligent callback scheduling for no-answers and "call me later" responses
- Compliance with Do Not Call registry checks before dialing
- A/B testing of different opening scripts and offer framings
- Real-time call monitoring and whisper/barge capability for human managers

### Data Inputs Required
- Lead list with name, phone number, and any enrichment data
- Offer/product description and key value propositions
- Qualification criteria (what makes a good lead)
- Objection library with approved responses
- Calendar availability for appointment booking
- CRM access for disposition logging

### Integration Points
- GHL, Salesforce, HubSpot, Pipedrive for lead management and disposition
- Google Calendar / Outlook / Calendly for live booking
- Vapi or Bland.ai for voice infrastructure
- DNC registry API for compliance screening
- Twilio for call routing and recording
- Zapier/Make for post-call workflow triggers (notify sales rep, send confirmation email, etc.)

### Sample Prompt/Persona
> "You are Jordan, an outbound sales specialist for [Company Name]. You are calling [Prospect First Name] because they recently expressed interest in [Offer/Service]. Your tone is confident, friendly, and consultative — never pushy. Your goal is to understand if [Company Name] can genuinely help them, and if so, to schedule a 15-minute call with one of our specialists. If they object, acknowledge it sincerely and ask a clarifying question before responding. If they say they're not interested, thank them politely and ask if it's okay to reach out in the future."

### Customization Variables
- Target lead persona and vertical
- Offer details, pricing, and value proposition language
- Qualification script and scoring thresholds
- Objection responses per product/service
- Call pacing (how aggressively to dial per hour)
- Calendar to book into and appointment type
- Disposition labels per client CRM schema

### Stickiness Factor
A client running 200+ dials per day through this system generates a consistent, trackable lead flow that becomes a core revenue driver — turning it off means pipeline immediately dries up, creating a high switching cost.

### Upsell Path
Pairs with SKILL-010 (SMS Lead Nurture Agent) for multi-touch sequences, SKILL-017 (Lead Scoring Engine) to prioritize the dial list, and SKILL-027 (Email Campaign Builder) for pre-call warm-up sequences.

## SKILL-003: AI Appointment Reminder Agent

**Category**: Voice Agent
**Applies To**: All Industries
**Deployment Platform**: Vapi, GHL, Twilio, Make
**Complexity**: Low
**Monthly Value to Client**: $400 - $1,200

### What It Does
This AI employee proactively contacts confirmed appointment holders before their scheduled time via voice call, SMS, or both — confirming attendance, surfacing rescheduling needs early, and filling cancellation slots with waitlisted clients. It eliminates no-shows and last-minute cancellations, which for service businesses represent a direct and measurable revenue recovery. The agent handles the full confirmation or rescheduling interaction without any human involvement.

### Core Capabilities
- Automated outbound reminder calls and/or SMS at configurable intervals (e.g., 48 hours, 24 hours, 2 hours before)
- Two-way voice or SMS conversation to confirm, cancel, or reschedule
- Real-time calendar update when client reschedules or cancels
- Automatic backfill notification to waitlist when a slot opens
- Staff notification when a cancellation occurs
- Personalized message with appointment specifics (date, time, provider, location, address)
- Confirmation logging to CRM with response captured
- Repeat reminders if no confirmation received
- Custom pre-appointment instructions (what to bring, fasting requirements, parking info, etc.)

### Data Inputs Required
- Calendar/scheduling system with upcoming appointments
- Client contact information (phone, SMS preference)
- Business address and pre-appointment instructions
- Waitlist (if applicable)
- Staff notification preferences

### Integration Points
- Calendly, Acuity, Jane App, Mindbody, Zocdoc, Google Calendar, Outlook
- GHL or HubSpot for contact record updates
- Twilio for SMS delivery
- Vapi for voice reminder calls
- Zapier/Make to trigger waitlist fill logic

### Sample Prompt/Persona
> "Hi [Client Name], this is a reminder from [Business Name] about your upcoming appointment with [Provider] on [Date] at [Time] at [Location]. To confirm your appointment, press 1 or say 'confirm.' To reschedule, press 2 or say 'reschedule.' To cancel, press 3 or say 'cancel.' If you need to speak with our team directly, press 0. Is there anything you need us to know before your visit?"

### Customization Variables
- Reminder timing and frequency (1 call, 2 calls, SMS + call, etc.)
- Voice tone and persona name
- Pre-appointment instructions by appointment type
- Cancellation policy language
- Rescheduling availability window
- Waitlist logic and notification rules
- Industry-specific terminology (appointment vs. session vs. visit vs. reservation)

### Stickiness Factor
Once no-show rates drop measurably (commonly 30-60% reduction), the client sees direct revenue recovery and will attribute it to the system — no one wants to return to manual confirmation calls.

### Upsell Path
Pairs with SKILL-004 (Review Request Agent) to trigger a review request post-visit, SKILL-001 (AI Receptionist) for clients who also need inbound handling, and SKILL-015 (AI Booking Bot) for self-service rescheduling.

## SKILL-004: AI Review Request Agent

**Category**: Voice Agent
**Applies To**: All Industries
**Deployment Platform**: Vapi, GHL, Twilio
**Complexity**: Low
**Monthly Value to Client**: $500 - $1,500

### What It Does
This AI employee contacts customers within hours of a completed service or transaction — by call or text — to gauge satisfaction and, if the response is positive, guide them to leave a Google or Yelp review with a direct link. If sentiment is negative, it captures the complaint and routes it to an internal team member before the client posts publicly, effectively acting as a reputation shield. This agent systematically grows review count, star rating, and local SEO authority on autopilot.

### Core Capabilities
- Post-service triggered outreach via voice or SMS (configurable delay window)
- Sentiment detection in real-time during call or via text response
- Positive path: delivers review link via SMS and coaches the client on what to include
- Negative path: captures complaint details and immediately alerts the owner/manager
- Review link generation for Google Business Profile, Yelp, Facebook, or any platform
- Response rate tracking and analytics dashboard
- Suppression of review requests for flagged or problematic customers
- Customizable satisfaction questions (single question or multi-step NPS flow)
- Automatic re-trigger if client didn't open the link within 24 hours (configurable)

### Data Inputs Required
- Customer contact data and job/service completion trigger
- Google Business Profile or review platform URLs
- List of suppressed contacts (if any)
- Owner/manager alert contact info for escalation

### Integration Points
- GHL, ServiceTitan, Jobber, HubSpot for service completion triggers
- Google Business Profile API for review link generation
- Twilio for SMS delivery
- Vapi for voice calls
- Zapier/Make for workflow orchestration
- Slack or email for negative review escalation alerts

### Sample Prompt/Persona
> "Hi [Customer Name], this is [Agent Name] from [Business Name] — we just finished up your [Service] today and wanted to make sure everything met your expectations. How would you rate your experience on a scale of 1 to 10? ... [If 8+]: That's wonderful to hear! Would you be willing to share that experience in a quick Google review? It really helps our small business. I can text you a direct link right now — it only takes about 60 seconds. [If below 8]: I'm sorry to hear that. Can you tell me a little more about what happened? I'd like to make sure we make this right for you."

### Customization Variables
- Trigger timing after service completion (same day, next day, etc.)
- Review platform priority (Google vs. Yelp vs. Facebook)
- Satisfaction threshold for positive/negative routing (e.g., 7+)
- Number of re-trigger attempts
- Escalation contact and method (call, SMS, Slack, email)
- Industry-specific service language

### Stickiness Factor
Review velocity becomes a visible, measurable business metric — clients track their star rating and review count monthly, and the agent becomes directly credited for growth, making it psychologically difficult to remove.

### Upsell Path
Pairs naturally with SKILL-028 (Reputation & Review Manager) for responding to reviews, SKILL-006 (Survey & Feedback Collector) for deeper satisfaction data, and SKILL-030 (Local SEO & GBP Manager) for full Google presence management.

## SKILL-005: AI Customer Reactivation Agent

**Category**: Voice Agent
**Applies To**: All Industries
**Deployment Platform**: Vapi, GHL, Twilio
**Complexity**: Medium
**Monthly Value to Client**: $800 - $3,000

### What It Does
This AI employee mines a client's dormant customer database — anyone who hasn't purchased or booked in a defined period — and makes personalized outreach calls or sends texts to re-engage them with a relevant offer, update, or check-in. It is one of the highest-ROI activities a service business can run because the contacts already know and trust the brand. The agent qualifies reactivation interest, presents an offer, and books appointments directly, generating immediate revenue from a previously idle asset.

### Core Capabilities
- CRM segmentation to identify dormant customers by last contact date, service type, or spend level
- Personalized outreach referencing past services or purchases to create relevance
- Offer presentation (discount, new service, loyalty reward, seasonal promotion)
- Two-way conversation with objection handling for "not right now" responses
- Live appointment booking for re-engaged clients
- Automated follow-up sequence (call → text → email) for non-responders
- Suppression of opt-outs and DNC-flagged contacts
- Revenue attribution tracking (how much $ reactivated per campaign run)
- Segmented campaigns by customer type, service history, or geography

### Data Inputs Required
- Customer database with last interaction date and contact history
- Service/purchase history per customer
- Current offer or promotion to present
- Calendar availability for booking
- Opt-out and DNC list

### Integration Points
- GHL, HubSpot, Salesforce, Keap, Jobber for customer data
- Google Calendar / scheduling platform for booking
- Twilio for outbound SMS/calls
- Vapi for voice calls
- Zapier/Make for campaign scheduling and triggers

### Sample Prompt/Persona
> "Hi [Customer Name], this is [Agent Name] calling from [Business Name]. We actually helped you with [Last Service] back in [Month/Year] and we just wanted to check in and see how things are going. We haven't connected in a while and we're currently offering existing customers [Specific Offer]. I know you're busy, so I'll keep this short — would that be something that might be useful for you right now?"

### Customization Variables
- Dormancy threshold (60 days, 90 days, 6 months, 12 months)
- Offer or promotion details
- Personalization depth (reference specific past service, provider, or date)
- Campaign frequency cap (how often to re-contact an unresponsive customer)
- Revenue attribution tracking fields
- Segmentation logic (high-value customers first, by geography, by service type)

### Stickiness Factor
When a single reactivation campaign generates $5,000–$50,000+ in booked revenue from a previously idle database, the client sees an immediate, undeniable return on the subscription fee and rarely cancels.

### Upsell Path
Pairs with SKILL-010 (SMS Lead Nurture Agent) for multi-touch text sequences, SKILL-027 (Email Campaign Builder) for email reactivation layer, and SKILL-033 (Revenue Dashboard Agent) to track campaign-attributed revenue.

## SKILL-006: AI Survey & Feedback Collector

**Category**: Voice Agent
**Applies To**: All Industries
**Deployment Platform**: Vapi, GHL, Twilio, Make
**Complexity**: Low
**Monthly Value to Client**: $400 - $1,000

### What It Does
This AI employee conducts structured post-service satisfaction surveys via outbound phone call, capturing rich verbal feedback that text surveys simply cannot match. It follows a configurable question flow, listens and responds dynamically, handles emotional responses with empathy, and synthesizes results into structured data for reporting. The agent gives business owners a continuous stream of actionable voice-of-customer data without requiring a single manual call.

### Core Capabilities
- Outbound survey calling triggered by service completion, appointment end, or purchase event
- Configurable question bank (NPS, CSAT, specific service questions, open-ended)
- Dynamic follow-up questions based on responses (adaptive survey logic)
- Sentiment analysis of verbal responses with scoring
- Verbatim transcript capture of all responses
- Aggregated reporting dashboard with trend analysis
- Escalation flag when critical feedback is captured (low scores, complaints)
- Multiple survey length options (60-second quick pulse vs. 5-minute deep dive)
- SMS survey alternative with branching logic for non-phone responses
- Integration with product/service improvement workflows

### Data Inputs Required
- Survey question set and branching logic
- Service completion triggers with customer contact info
- Benchmark scores if tracking improvement over time
- Escalation contact for critical feedback
- Historical survey data for comparison (optional)

### Integration Points
- GHL, HubSpot, Salesforce for trigger events and response logging
- Vapi or Twilio for outbound voice
- Typeform or SurveyMonkey for SMS/web fallback
- Google Sheets or Airtable for raw response storage
- Looker Studio / Power BI for dashboard reporting
- Slack or email for escalation alerts

### Sample Prompt/Persona
> "Hello [Customer Name], I'm calling on behalf of [Business Name] to ask a few quick questions about your recent experience with us. This will only take about two minutes and your feedback genuinely shapes how we improve. First question: overall, how would you rate your experience from 1 to 10? ... [Dynamic follow-up based on score]. Is there one thing we could have done better or that you'd like to highlight? Your honest answer truly helps us. Thank you for your time — we really appreciate your business."

### Customization Variables
- Survey length and number of questions
- Question library (NPS, CSAT, effort score, open-ended)
- Trigger conditions and delay after service
- Scoring thresholds for escalation
- Reporting cadence (weekly digest, real-time, monthly)
- Suppression rules for repeat surveying

### Stickiness Factor
Survey data becomes a KPI that leadership tracks — when NPS or CSAT trends are visible in a dashboard, the survey engine becomes an operational asset embedded in performance review cycles.

### Upsell Path
Pairs with SKILL-004 (Review Request Agent) to convert happy survey respondents into reviewers, SKILL-033 (Revenue Dashboard Agent) to correlate satisfaction with retention, and SKILL-028 (Reputation Manager) to respond to any issues surfaced.

## SKILL-007: AI Payment Reminder Agent

**Category**: Voice Agent
**Applies To**: All Industries
**Deployment Platform**: Vapi, GHL, Twilio, Make
**Complexity**: Medium
**Monthly Value to Client**: $600 - $2,000

### What It Does
This AI employee handles the uncomfortable but critical task of contacting customers with overdue invoices or unpaid balances — via call or text — in a professional, non-confrontational way that maintains the client relationship while recovering revenue. It offers payment plans, accepts partial payments, collects payment method info for processing, and escalates unresolved cases to a human collections handler. It transforms accounts receivable follow-up from a dreaded manual task into a systematic, consistent process.

### Core Capabilities
- Automated outbound calls and texts for overdue invoices at configured intervals (3, 7, 14, 30 days past due)
- Friendly but firm scripting calibrated to days overdue (escalating tone over time)
- Payment plan negotiation within predefined parameters (e.g., split into 2-3 installments)
- Secure payment link delivery via SMS during or after the call
- Partial payment acceptance and balance tracking
- Promise-to-pay logging with automatic follow-up if payment doesn't arrive
- Dispute capture and routing to billing department
- Opt-out and hardship flag handling
- Escalation to human collections after a defined number of failed attempts
- Integration with accounting system to auto-mark invoices paid upon confirmation

### Data Inputs Required
- Accounts receivable data: customer name, phone, invoice amount, due date, days overdue
- Approved payment plan parameters (max installments, minimum payment)
- Payment gateway for link generation
- Escalation contacts and thresholds
- Accounting system integration credentials

### Integration Points
- QuickBooks, Xero, FreshBooks, Wave for invoice data
- Stripe, Square, PaySimple for payment link generation
- GHL or HubSpot for contact logging
- Twilio for outbound SMS/voice
- Vapi for voice AI calls
- Zapier/Make for invoice status updates after payment

### Sample Prompt/Persona
> "Hello [Customer Name], this is [Agent Name] calling from [Business Name]. I'm reaching out about invoice #[Invoice Number] for [Amount], which was due on [Date]. We want to make sure there isn't an issue and get this resolved easily for you. Are you able to process this payment today? ... [If no]: No problem — we do have a payment plan option. Would it help to split this into two payments? I can send you a secure payment link by text right now so you can take care of it at your convenience."

### Customization Variables
- Invoice aging thresholds and escalation schedule
- Script tone calibration (gentle reminder vs. firm final notice)
- Payment plan parameters (installments, minimum %, timeline)
- Payment gateway and link template
- Escalation triggers ($ amount, number of attempts)
- Industry-specific invoice language and terms

### Stickiness Factor
AR recovery becomes measurable — clients track collected vs. outstanding by week, and the agent often pays for itself within the first recovery campaign alone, creating a locked-in ROI narrative.

### Upsell Path
Pairs with SKILL-020 (Document Generator) for invoice and payment plan agreement generation, SKILL-031 (Invoice Processing Agent) for AP side, and SKILL-033 (Revenue Dashboard Agent) for AR aging visibility.

## SKILL-008: AI After-Hours Emergency Triage Agent

**Category**: Voice Agent
**Applies To**: All Industries
**Deployment Platform**: Vapi, GHL, Twilio
**Complexity**: Medium
**Monthly Value to Client**: $600 - $1,800

### What It Does
This AI employee is the safety net that catches every call that comes in outside of business hours — nights, weekends, and holidays. Rather than sending callers to voicemail, it conducts a live triage conversation to determine the urgency of the situation: genuine emergencies get routed to an on-call human immediately; routine requests are logged and scheduled for next-business-day follow-up. It protects the business from missed emergencies while protecting staff from unnecessary after-hours interruptions.

### Core Capabilities
- 24/7 live answer with human-like voice conversation
- Urgency classification engine (emergency vs. urgent vs. routine vs. spam)
- Emergency escalation: immediate live transfer or SMS/call alert to on-call staff
- Non-emergency: message capture, callback scheduling, and automated acknowledgment to caller
- Industry-specific emergency criteria (medical, HVAC, legal, property management, etc.)
- On-call rotation awareness (routes to the right staff member based on the day/time)
- Caller ID lookup in CRM to personalize response
- Full call transcript and recording delivered to team at start of next business day
- SLA timer for emergency response tracking
- Bilingual support for emergency calls

### Data Inputs Required
- Business hours definition
- Emergency vs. non-emergency classification criteria
- On-call staff schedule and contact numbers
- CRM access for caller lookup
- After-hours message template

### Integration Points
- GHL, Salesforce, HubSpot for caller lookup and ticket creation
- Vapi or Twilio for call routing and live transfer
- PagerDuty, OpsGenie, or SMS for on-call alerts
- Google Calendar for on-call rotation schedule
- Zapier/Make for next-day follow-up workflow triggers

### Sample Prompt/Persona
> "Thank you for calling [Business Name]. Our office is currently closed, but I'm here to help. My name is [Agent Name]. Can I ask what's going on so I can make sure you get the right assistance? ... [If emergency]: I understand — this sounds urgent. I'm going to connect you with our on-call team member right now. Please stay on the line. [If non-emergency]: Got it. I'll make sure the right person gets your message first thing tomorrow morning and follows up with you. Can I confirm the best number to reach you?"

### Customization Variables
- Emergency classification rules by industry (e.g., "no heat" for HVAC = emergency; "billing question" = not)
- On-call rotation schedule and staff contacts
- Business hours definition and holiday schedule
- Acknowledgment message for non-emergency callers
- SLA response time targets for different urgency levels
- After-hours behavior differences (weeknight vs. weekend vs. holiday)

### Stickiness Factor

- Do Not Call (DNC) registry scrubbing before every outbound campaign
- GDPR data subject request handling: right to access, right to erasure, data portability
- Consent expiry tracking (GDPR-compliant re-consent campaigns when consent ages)
- State-specific restriction enforcement (e.g., California CPPA, Florida SB 1120)
- Prior express written consent documentation for autodialer campaigns
- Compliance audit report generation for legal review or regulatory inquiry
- Real-time blocking of non-compliant outreach attempts with alert logging

### Data Inputs Required

- Campaign outreach lists before execution
- DNC registry access (FTC, state-level)

### Integration Points
