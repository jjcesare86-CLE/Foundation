## SKILL-121: AI Pain Assessment Intake Widget

**Category**: Widget/Tool
**Applies To**: Chiropractic, Physical Therapy, Pain Management, Wellness
**Deployment Platform**: Custom (Embedded Web Widget), GHL, EHR Integrations
**Complexity**: Medium
**Monthly Value to Client**: $500 - $1,200

### What It Does
An interactive digital body map widget embedded on the practice website or patient portal where patients tap the areas where they feel pain, rate severity on a 0–10 scale, and describe symptom onset, frequency, and character (sharp, dull, radiating). The AI synthesizes this data into a structured intake summary delivered to the provider before the appointment begins, dramatically reducing paper-form time and improving clinical readiness.

### Core Capabilities
- Interactive front/back/side body map with tap-to-select pain zones
- Severity rating slider (0–10 VAS scale) per pain zone
- Symptom descriptor prompts: onset date, duration, triggers, relieving factors, radiation pattern
- AI-generated pre-visit summary in clinical language (SOAP-style)
- Automatic red-flag screening (chest pain, numbness/weakness, bowel/bladder changes) with provider alert
- Condition history pre-population for returning patients
- Intake data pushed to EHR (ChiroTouch, EHRchive, Jane App) or GHL contact record
- Completion reminders via SMS if patient starts but doesn't finish

### Data Inputs Required
- Patient-selected body map locations
- Pain severity ratings (0–10 per zone)
- Symptom descriptions (typed or voice-to-text)
- Onset/duration/frequency responses
- Prior injury or condition history (optional pull from patient record)

### Integration Points
- EHR systems: ChiroTouch, Jane App, EHRchive, Genesis Chiropractic
- Practice management: GHL, Mindbody
- SMS/Email: Twilio, SendGrid for completion reminders
- Provider dashboard: real-time intake notification

### Sample Prompt/Persona
> "I'm your intake assistant. Before your visit, I'd like to understand where you're experiencing discomfort. Tap the areas on the body map, then I'll ask a few quick follow-up questions so your provider has everything they need when you arrive. This takes about 3–5 minutes."

### Customization Variables
- `{practice_name}` — branded widget header
- `{provider_name}` — assigned reviewing provider
- `{red_flag_conditions}` — configurable alert triggers
- `{ehr_system}` — target integration
- `{appointment_type}` — new patient vs. follow-up flow variant
- `{language}` — multilingual support toggle

### Stickiness Factor
Embedded in the pre-appointment patient journey, this becomes a standard workflow requirement. Providers who see structured AI intake summaries resist returning to paper forms. Connects to every new patient booking, creating daily recurring value.

### Upsell Path
→ SKILL-122 (Exercise Program Delivery) for home care follow-through
→ SKILL-123 (Ergonomic Assessment) for workplace injury cases
→ SKILL-124 (Personal Injury Case Coordinator) for PI caseloads
→ Full patient journey automation package ($2,000–$4,000/mo)

## SKILL-122: AI Exercise & Stretching Program Delivery

**Category**: Content Engine
**Applies To**: Chiropractic, Physical Therapy, Sports Medicine, Wellness
**Deployment Platform**: Custom Web Portal, GHL, SMS/Email Automation
**Complexity**: Medium
**Monthly Value to Client**: $600 - $1,400

### What It Does
Generates personalized home exercise programs (HEPs) based on a patient's diagnosed condition, treatment phase, fitness level, and available equipment. The AI assembles exercise sequences with video demonstrations, written cue instructions, sets/reps/frequency parameters, and delivers them via the patient's preferred channel (SMS link, email, patient portal). Program progression is automated as treatment milestones are reached.

### Core Capabilities
- Condition-based exercise library (lumbar, cervical, shoulder, hip, postural, etc.) with 200+ movements
- AI program builder: selects exercises, sets parameters, sequences warm-up → main → cool-down
- Video demonstration library integrated with each exercise card
- Multi-phase program progression (acute → sub-acute → maintenance)
- Patient completion tracking with check-in prompts ("Did you complete today's exercises? How did they feel?")
- Compliance reporting sent to provider weekly
- Modification suggestions based on patient feedback (too hard, too easy, caused pain)
- Printable PDF + digital mobile-friendly format

### Data Inputs Required
- Diagnosed condition / chief complaint
- Treatment phase (acute, sub-acute, chronic, maintenance)
- Patient fitness level and any contraindications
- Available home equipment (none, resistance bands, weights, etc.)
- Frequency of in-office visits
- Provider-specified exercise preferences or exclusions

### Integration Points
- EHR: ChiroTouch, Jane App for condition/treatment data pull
- SMS: Twilio for program delivery and check-in messages
- Email: SendGrid / GHL email automation
- Video hosting: Vimeo or YouTube (unlisted) for exercise demos
- GHL: patient compliance tracking in contact timeline

### Sample Prompt/Persona
> "Hi [Patient Name], here's your home exercise program from Dr. [Provider]. These 4 exercises are designed specifically for your lower back. Each one has a video demonstration — tap to watch. Complete this routine every day for best results. After 7 days, I'll check in to see how you're doing and update your program as needed."

### Customization Variables
- `{condition}` — pulls relevant exercise categories
- `{phase}` — acute/sub-acute/maintenance program variant
- `{frequency}` — daily, 3x/week, etc.
- `{equipment_available}` — filters exercise options
- `{provider_preferences}` — exclude specific movements
- `{patient_name}` and `{clinic_branding}` — personalization

### Stickiness Factor
Patients on active HEP programs receive daily digital touchpoints from the practice. Compliance data flows back to the provider, creating a clinical feedback loop that justifies continued use. The more conditions treated, the more indispensable the exercise library becomes.

### Upsell Path
→ SKILL-121 (Pain Assessment Intake) for new patient onboarding
→ SKILL-123 (Ergonomic Assessment) for preventive program layer
→ Nutrition and wellness content delivery add-on
→ Telehealth check-in integration

## SKILL-123: AI Ergonomic Assessment Tool

**Category**: Widget/Tool
**Applies To**: Chiropractic, Occupational Health, Physical Therapy, Corporate Wellness
**Deployment Platform**: Custom Web Widget, GHL, Zapier
**Complexity**: Medium
**Monthly Value to Client**: $400 - $900

### What It Does
An interactive questionnaire and photo-guided assessment tool that evaluates a patient's or employee's workstation setup — desk height, monitor position, chair adjustment, keyboard/mouse placement, lighting, and posture habits. The AI cross-references responses against ergonomic best practices and generates a personalized report with a risk score, prioritized recommendations, and optional product suggestions. Deployed both for patient home offices and as a B2B corporate wellness tool.

### Core Capabilities
- 25-question guided workspace assessment (chair, desk, monitor, keyboard, lighting, break habits)
- Optional photo upload for AI posture and setup analysis
- Ergonomic risk score by body zone (neck, lower back, wrists, eyes)
- Prioritized recommendation report ranked by impact and ease of implementation
- Product recommendation cards (ergonomic chairs, monitor stands, wrist rests) with affiliate links optional
- Corporate bulk assessment portal with department-level analytics
- Follow-up scheduling prompt linked to practice booking system
- PDF report auto-generated and emailed to patient/employee

### Data Inputs Required
- Workstation setup questionnaire responses
- Optional workspace photos
- Reported pain areas (cross-referenced with SKILL-121 if integrated)
- Job type and daily screen/desk hours
- Current symptoms and duration

### Integration Points
- GHL: assessment lead capture + follow-up sequences
- EHR: assessment results pushed to patient record
- Email: PDF report delivery via SendGrid
- Calendar: appointment booking integration (Calendly, Jane App)
- Corporate HR platforms: BambooHR, Workday (API for bulk deployment)

### Sample Prompt/Persona
> "Let's evaluate your workspace to identify what might be contributing to your discomfort. I'll ask you about 15 questions about your setup, and if you can share a quick photo of your workstation, I can give you even more specific recommendations. This takes about 5 minutes and you'll receive a personalized report immediately."

### Customization Variables
- `{assessment_context}` — patient self-assessment vs. corporate HR vs. post-injury
- `{practice_branding}` — white-labeled with clinic logo and colors
- `{affiliate_products}` — product recommendation links (on/off toggle)
- `{follow_up_trigger}` — auto-book appointment if risk score above threshold
- `{corporate_account}` — bulk deployment for employer client

### Stickiness Factor
Corporate clients deploy this across entire workforces, creating recurring assessment cycles annually or after job changes. Patient-side, it's a differentiating amenity that drives both initial visits and preventive care re-engagement.

### Upsell Path
→ Corporate wellness retainer program (monthly reporting, $1,500–$5,000/mo)
→ SKILL-122 (Exercise Program Delivery) for corrective exercise add-on
→ Lunch-and-learn webinar automation for HR departments
→ Workers' compensation referral pipeline

## SKILL-124: AI Personal Injury Case Coordinator

**Category**: Operations
**Applies To**: Chiropractic, Physical Therapy, Pain Management (PI Caseload)
**Deployment Platform**: GHL, Custom, Zapier, Make
**Complexity**: High
**Monthly Value to Client**: $1,500 - $3,500

### What It Does
Manages the full lifecycle of personal injury patients from first contact through case settlement — tracking treatment progress, generating medical lien letters, maintaining attorney communication logs, producing demand package documentation, and alerting staff to critical case deadlines. The AI acts as a virtual PI case manager, reducing administrative burden while ensuring no billing opportunity or legal deadline is missed.

### Core Capabilities
- PI patient intake: accident date, mechanism of injury, insurance info, attorney name/firm
- Automated lien letter generation (customizable templates per jurisdiction)
- Treatment timeline tracker: visit count, missed appointments, gap-in-care flags
- Attorney communication portal: status update emails/texts on schedule (weekly, monthly, or milestone-triggered)
- Medical records request tracker with follow-up automation
- Maximum Medical Improvement (MMI) milestone alert and documentation trigger
- Demand package checklist: ensures all records, bills, and narratives are compiled
- Balance outstanding tracker by case with attorney firm reporting

### Data Inputs Required
- Patient accident details (date, type, at-fault party, insurance info)
- Attorney firm contact information
- EHR visit records and billing data
- Lien amounts and payment history
- Jurisdiction-specific legal requirements

### Integration Points
- EHR: ChiroTouch, Practice Fusion for visit/billing data
- GHL: case pipeline management, attorney contact records
- Document generation: PandaDoc, DocuSign for lien letters
- Email/SMS: attorney communication automation
- Practice management billing: for lien balance tracking

### Sample Prompt/Persona
> "I'm managing the coordination for [Patient Name]'s personal injury case. I track their treatment progress, maintain communication with your office and their attorney [Firm Name], and ensure all documentation is ready for demand. You'll receive automatic updates at each key milestone, and I'll alert you if there's a gap in care or upcoming deadline that needs attention."

### Customization Variables
- `{jurisdiction}` — state-specific lien laws and documentation requirements
- `{lien_language}` — customizable lien letter templates
- `{attorney_update_frequency}` — weekly, biweekly, milestone-based
- `{mmm_threshold}` — visit count or weeks to trigger MMI review
- `{billing_thresholds}` — alert when case balance exceeds set amounts

### Stickiness Factor
PI coordinators save 10–15 hours per week per practice. Once the workflow is built around this system — with attorney relationships managed through it — the switching cost is extremely high. As caseloads grow, value compounds.

### Upsell Path
→ SKILL-131 (Insurance Claim Assistance) for multi-payer PI cases
→ Medical-legal report drafting assistant
→ Attorney referral tracking CRM module
→ Full PI practice management suite

## SKILL-125: AI Photo-Based Pest Identification Tool

**Category**: Widget/Tool
**Applies To**: Pest Control, Extermination Services
**Deployment Platform**: Custom Web Widget, GHL, SMS/MMS
**Complexity**: Medium
**Monthly Value to Client**: $400 - $900

### What It Does
A customer-facing widget on the pest control company's website that allows homeowners or business owners to upload a photo of an insect, rodent, damage pattern, or infestation sign. The AI identifies the pest species with confidence rating, explains the associated risks, describes the typical treatment approach, and prompts the customer to book a free inspection or service call — converting curiosity into qualified leads automatically.

### Core Capabilities
- Image recognition for 150+ common pest species (insects, rodents, wildlife, fungal damage)
- Confidence-scored identification with "likely" and "alternative" possibilities
- Risk and damage explanation per identified pest
- Recommended treatment type (chemical, heat, exclusion, trapping, fumigation)
- Urgency scoring: routine vs. time-sensitive vs. emergency
- Instant quote range based on pest type and typical treatment protocol
- Lead capture gated at identification results or post-recommendation
- Identified pest data pushed to GHL contact record for sales follow-up

### Data Inputs Required
- Customer-uploaded photo (JPEG/PNG/HEIC)
- Location (zip code for regional pest seasonality context)
- Where pest was found (kitchen, basement, exterior, attic, etc.)
- How long problem has been noticed
- Prior treatments applied

### Integration Points
- GHL: lead capture and pipeline entry with pest identified
- Booking: Calendly, Jobber, ServiceTitan for inspection scheduling
- SMS/Email: Twilio/SendGrid for follow-up sequences
- CRM: pest type tagged to contact for targeted campaign segmentation
- Payment: estimate-to-job workflow integration

### Sample Prompt/Persona
> "Upload a clear photo of the pest or damage you've spotted and I'll identify what you're dealing with in seconds. Based on what I find, I'll explain the risk level, what treatment is typically needed, and you can book a free inspection right from here — no commitment required."

### Customization Variables
- `{service_area}` — regional pest species weighting
- `{urgency_thresholds}` — which pests trigger emergency call routing
- `{pricing_ranges}` — estimate ranges per treatment type
- `{booking_cta}` — inspection vs. quote vs. call routing
- `{seasonal_alerts}` — seasonal pest campaigns (termite swarm season, etc.)

### Stickiness Factor
Lead quality from photo-identified pest inquiries is dramatically higher than generic contact forms — the prospect has already self-qualified. The identification widget becomes the #1 lead source for many clients, making it irreplaceable.

### Upsell Path
→ SKILL-126 (Recurring Service Schedule Manager) for converting one-time to subscription
→ SKILL-127 (Commercial Multi-Location Portal) for B2B pest accounts
→ Seasonal pest prevention campaign automation
→ Neighborhood targeted campaign triggered by identified infestations

### Core Capabilities

- Damage probability scoring: based on storm severity, hail size, housing stock age

### Data Inputs Required
- Service area zip codes
- Weather API feed (Tomorrow.io, Weather.com API, NOAA)
- Homeowner contact lists by zip code (data provider integration)
- Roofing company capacity (max inspections per day)
- Prior storm campaign performance data

### Integration Points

### Customization Variables

- `{outreach_channels}` — SMS only vs. SMS + voice + email

### Stickiness Factor
Storm season is the roofing industry's make-or-break revenue period. Being the first company in a neighborhood post-storm is worth $50,000–$500,000 in jobs. This tool becomes the client's competitive edge in their highest-revenue window.

### Upsell Path
