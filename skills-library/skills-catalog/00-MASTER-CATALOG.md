# Automation Nation — AI Skills & Sub-Agents Master Catalog

## 165 Deployable AI Employee Skills Across 30+ Industries

This catalog is the foundational product shelf for Automation Nation. Your voice agent in Claude pulls skills from this catalog, customizes them per client, and delivers AI employee solutions.

---

## How This Catalog Works

1. **Client Discovery**: Your voice agent asks the client about their industry and pain points
2. **Skill Selection**: The agent identifies relevant skills from this catalog (universal + industry-specific)
3. **Customization**: Each skill has documented customization variables — the agent adjusts for the client's specific business
4. **Package Building**: Skills are bundled into Starter / Professional / Enterprise tiers
5. **Deployment**: Skills are built out on Vapi, GHL, Claude, and supporting platforms
